Operational language

One verdict system across pre-send and recovery flows.

Verdict
SAFE

Approve payout

Expected details match the provided route and the destination does not trigger a material warning.

Verdict
BLOCK

Stop before funds move

Used for clear mismatches, invalid address formats, or an obviously wrong transfer route.

Verdict
REVERIFY

Pause and confirm

Used when a destination or context needs human confirmation before approval.

Verdict
TEST FIRST

Use a low-risk trial

Used for exchange-like deposits, bridge-router behavior, or other cases where a small test is safer than a full send.

confidenceA useful summary layer when the verdict needs nuance.
destination typePersonal wallet, contract, exchange-like deposit, bridge-router, or unknown.
why this verdictA short, human-readable explanation for ops and support teams.
Pilot flow

How PayeeProof fits into a real workflow.

1

Pre-send request

A payout system sends expected and provided transfer details before approval.

2

Verdict + record

PayeeProof returns a structured verdict and a readable verification record for workflow storage.

3

Escalation only when needed

Support or ops only intervenes when the answer is BLOCK, REVERIFY, TEST FIRST, or a recovery case.
